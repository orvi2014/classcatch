Extension Options

classCatch is configured through the extension popup. Click the classCatch icon in your browser toolbar to access all settings.

From the popup, you can:

  • Enable or disable the extension
  • Change the theme (dark/light)
  • Set the mode (auto/tailwind/css/convert)
  • Verify your license to unlock Pro features
